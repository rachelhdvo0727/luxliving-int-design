FAQ

  • 1.How do I change my current subscription?

    You can go to your Account Settings and change it. The new subscription will be in affect the following month.

    Simply click on your profil picture at the top right corner of website and then:
    Account Settings → click on "Subscription" section → click on "Change subscription"

    If you change it after the 15th of the month, the new subscription will not be applied the following month but the month after that.

  • 2.How do I put my subscription on pause?

    You can go to your Account Settings and change it. The change will take affect the following month.

    Simply click on your profil picture at the top right corner of website and then:
    Account Settings → click on "Subscription" section → click on "Set on pause"

  • 3.How do I send my writings to the magazine?

    As a reader, you can also send us your writings on interior design and other design topics.
    You can send us your writings to the following email: writer@luxliving.com

  • 4.How do I change frequency of receiving newsletters?

    You can change how often LuxLiving communicate with you by customizing your communication preferences in Account Settings

    Simply click on your profil picture at the top right corner of website and then:
    Account Settings → click on "Communication" section

  • 5. Can I delete my account?

    Yes, you can, even though we are sad to see you go.

    After 14 days, your account will be totally deleted from our database. You will receive a confirmation email from us.

    The "Delete" button can be found by going through these following steps:
    Account Settings → click on "Account" section
